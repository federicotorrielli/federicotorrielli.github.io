## Course Overview

An intensive 18-hour practical course focused on real-world applications of Generative AI and Large Language Models in business contexts. The course emphasizes hands-on implementation, practical prompt engineering, and safe, responsible AI deployment.

---

## Details

**Organization**: Alpha Consultants, Italy  
**Format**: In-house training  
**Duration**: 18 hours  
**Date Range**: January - February 2026  
**Audience**: Enterprise staff and consultants

---

## Course Topics

- **Generative AI & LLM Fundamentals**: Core concepts and practical understanding
- **Prompt Engineering Techniques**: Advanced strategies for enterprise applications
- **Business Use Cases**: Real-world applications and case studies
- **Integration Strategies**: How to effectively integrate AI tools into business workflows
- **AI Safety and Responsible Use**: Best practices for enterprise deployment
- **Hands-on Practice**: Practical exercises and live demonstrations
- **Performance Optimization**: Techniques for maximizing AI tool effectiveness

---

## Key Learning Outcomes

Participants will learn:

- How to leverage generative AI for business problems
- Advanced prompt engineering techniques for optimal results
- How to evaluate and select appropriate AI tools
- Safe and responsible AI implementation practices
- How to measure and maximize ROI from AI adoption
- Real-world troubleshooting and optimization strategies
