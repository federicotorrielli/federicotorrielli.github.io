## Course Overview

A comprehensive 24-hour online course covering the essentials of Generative AI, Large Language Models, and practical applications for public administration professionals. The course progresses from foundational theory to hands-on prompt engineering practice and critical AI safety considerations.

---

## Details

**Institution**: Comune di Torino, Italy  
**Format**: Online course  
**Duration**: 24 hours  
**Date Range**: January - February 2026  
**Attendees**: 1,000 participants  
**Audience**: Public administration staff

---

## Course Topics

- **Generative AI Fundamentals**: Overview of generative AI models and their applications
- **Large Language Models**: Understanding how LLMs work, capabilities and limitations
- **Practical Prompt Engineering**: Techniques for effective prompt design and optimization
- **AI Safety**: Best practices for safe and responsible AI usage in public administration
- **Real-world Applications**: Practical use cases relevant to public administration workflows
- **Risk Management**: Understanding and mitigating AI-related risks

---

## Key Learning Outcomes

Participants will learn:

- How generative AI and LLMs function in practice
- How to craft effective prompts for various tasks
- How to implement AI safely and responsibly in their workflows
- Critical thinking about AI limitations and potential risks
- Practical applications specific to public administration contexts
